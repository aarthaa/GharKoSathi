How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our collection of gifts, select the item you want, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your delivery details and payment information.

Can I schedule a specific delivery time?

Yes, during the checkout process, you can specify a preferred delivery date and time. We'll do our best to accommodate your request, but please note that delivery slots may be subject to availability.
